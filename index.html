About Our Alumni Association

Our Mission

To foster lifelong connections between Daffodil International University and its alumni, creating a mutually beneficial network of support, mentorship, and continuous growth.

Our Vision

To build a globally recognized, highly engaged alumni community that actively contributes to the innovation, academic excellence, and heritage of DIU.

Our Objectives

Foster networking and mentorship, promote career growth, celebrate alumni achievements, and support current students through scholarships, talks, and internship pipelines.

Contribution to DIU

Alumni-funded labs, guest lectures, recruitment drives, and an annual homecoming that channels experience and resources back into the university that shaped them.
